We ship globally.

At Stella, we aim to provide seamless and efficient shipping for all your purchases. Below, you'll find details about our shipping process, delivery times, and related policies.



1. Shipping Locations

  • Domestic Shipping: We ship to all regions within the United States.
  • International Shipping: Available to select countries. Please check during checkout for availability.

2. Shipping Rates

  • Standard Shipping: Flat rate of $200 for domestic orders. Rates vary for international shipping.
  • Free Shipping: Not available at the moment. Please contact us for more information.
  • Express Shipping: Additional fees apply. Pricing displayed during checkout.

3. Delivery Times

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days (domestic).
  • Express Shipping: 1-3 business days (domestic).
  • International Shipping: 7-21 business days, depending on destination.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and may vary due to customs processing or unforeseen delays.


4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number and instructions on how to track your package. You can also track your order on our website at stella.space/track-order.


5. Shipping Restrictions

Certain items may have shipping restrictions due to size, weight, or destination-specific regulations. If your order is affected, we will notify you immediately.


6. Lost or Delayed Shipments

If your order is delayed or lost, please contact us at here2help@stella.space. We will assist in locating the shipment or issuing a replacement/refund as needed.


7. Returns and Refunds

For information on returning items or requesting a refund, please refer to our Returns and Refund Policy.


8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about shipping, feel free to contact us at:
